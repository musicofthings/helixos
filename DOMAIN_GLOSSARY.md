# Domain Glossary

Lab-domain terms used across HelixOS documentation and schemas. For how modules map to these concepts, see [docs/OVERVIEW.md](docs/OVERVIEW.md) and [docs/README.md](docs/README.md).

- **Aliquot**: A measured portion of a sample stored or used separately.
- **Audit event**: Immutable event describing who did what, when, and why.
- **Biobank**: Managed storage system for biological samples and metadata.
- **Construct**: Engineered DNA sequence or plasmid produced by a cloning workflow.
- **ELN**: Electronic Lab Notebook for experiment records and protocols.
- **Feature**: Annotated region on a biological sequence, such as promoter, CDS, or tag.
- **LIMS**: Laboratory Information Management System for sample and workflow tracking.
- **MCP connector**: Tool server exposing scientific capabilities to AI agents.
- **Organization**: Tenant boundary for users, data, billing, and permissions.
- **Plasmid**: Circular DNA molecule used in cloning and molecular biology.
- **Protocol**: Reusable scientific procedure with steps, reagents, and parameters.
- **Sample genealogy**: Parent-child provenance between specimens, aliquots, and derivatives.
- **Workspace**: Lab or team subdivision inside an organization.
